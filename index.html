Why Choose Us

ABC University was established in 2008 for the public benefit and it is recognized globally. Throughout our great history, we have offered access to a wide range of academic opportunities.

Our Mission

  • To encourage research of a high standard and ensure its dissemination to the community
  • To create an academic ecosystem of the sciences and the arts that is innovative and holistic in approach
  • To build an institution that is flexible and interdisciplinary in approach to knowledge
  • To ascertain that we cater to the needs of the wider society
  • To help shape professionals who are competitive within and outside the boundary of the country.

Core Values

In consonance with its motto Lakshya Viswamanam, the ABC University propagates five sets of Core Values to attain and disseminate the Five Es.

  • Creativity and Integrity
  • Inclusiveness and Diversity
  • Freedom and Belonging
  • Democracy and Tolerance
  • Compassion and Humanity

Our Vision

To strive for academic excellence that is accessible and inclusive to all.
